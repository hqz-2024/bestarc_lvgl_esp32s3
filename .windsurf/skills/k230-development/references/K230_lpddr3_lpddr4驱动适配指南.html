LPDDR4 swap#

lpddr4 swap说明#

建议：

LPDDR4 byte组内DQ可以swap，byte之间不支持DQ swap, channel 组内byte和byte支持swap。

建议按照参考板进行布线，自行设计时如果修改了swap，需要调整ddr驱动(traing)代码,修改方法参考下面章节。

理论要求：

  1. ODT, CK, CKE, CS can’t be swap.

  2. DQ can be swapped with any other DQ that in the same Byte

  3. For LPDDR4 the Byte swap can only occur within a channel, for LPDDR4, DBYTE0 could only be swapped with DBYTE1; DBYTE2 could only be swapped with DBYTE3, and it don’t need to modify Firmware;

  4. LPDDR4 byte组内DQ可以swap，byte之间不支持DQ swap, channel 组内byte和byte支持swap.

  5. DQS/DM must be swap along with the corresonding DBYTE swap. in the internal DBYTE, DQS/DM are not allowed swappend.

  6. For LPDDR4, CA could be swapped within the same channel,and should modify the Firmware to accommodate this.

调整规则#

调整规则见下图，左边寄存器基地址根据右边红色部分调整，左边bit位根据右边黄色部分调整，左边ca位号根据右边绿色部分调整。

ddr管脚和功能定义可以参考ddr相关管脚定义表

LPDDR3 DQ swap#

说明#

建议：

LPDDR3 byte组内DQ可以swap，byte之间不支持DQ swap, byte和byte不支持swap。

理论要求：

  1. ODT, CK, CKE, CS can’t be swap.

  2. DQ can be swapped with any other DQ that in the same Byte

  3. For LPDDR3, the four Dbytes could be swapped arbitrarily, and should modify the Firmware to accommodate this.

  4. DQS/DM must be swap along with the corresonding DBYTE swap. in the internal DBYTE, DQS/DM are not allowed swappend.

  5. For LPDDR3 CA pins could be swapped arbitrary.,and should modify the Firmware to accommodate this.

调整规则#

调整规则见下图，左边寄存器基地址根据右边红色部分调整，左边bit位根据右边黄色部分调整,左边ca位号根据右边绿色部分调整。
